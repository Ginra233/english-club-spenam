About Us

English Club SMPN 6 Cirebon is a community for students who are passionate about mastering English. We believe that learning should be an enjoyable process. Therefore, we focus on developing the four core skills: Listening, Speaking, Reading, and Writing through modern and interactive methods. Join us to gain confidence in communicating!

Our Activities

Storytelling

A fun activity to build confidence and public speaking skills. Each member will learn how to craft and deliver compelling stories in English.

Debate

Sharpening critical thinking and argumentation skills in English. We will discuss various interesting topics to practice logic and persuasive communication.

English Day

A special event to celebrate the English language. There will be various competitions, games, and performances, all conducted in English to make learning more enjoyable.

Our Learning Materials

We have an interactive curriculum designed to improve your English skills comprehensively. Some of the topics you will learn include:

Vocabulary & Grammar

Expanding your vocabulary and understanding grammatical rules for more effective communication.

Pronunciation & Conversation

Practicing correct pronunciation and building confidence in daily conversations.

Essay Writing & Public Speaking

Improving the ability to write essays and speak in public fluently.
